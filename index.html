About this Webinar

Welcome to our "Learn To Trade Shares & Stocks" video series. This series is the first out of two series on this topic where you will be introduced to one of the most common trading practices, trading shares & stocks. Here you will be introduced & displayed the basics of this topic in order to assist you to start your journey where most people feel more confident.


The reason why we chose this topic, is simply because that public traded shares & stocks are offered to us by companies which their services, goods or products are embedded in to our every day lives, for example; Apple, Microsoft, Netflix, Amazon. This helps in understanding more what is this all about as the information about this companies & their stocks is widely available for us all to find.
